About Us

Welcome to Elm Street Bakery - A Sweet Tradition in Everett, MA

At Elm Street Bakery, we believe every bite tells a story. Located in the heart of Everett, Massachusetts, our family-owned bakery has been bringing the community together with the comforting aroma of freshly baked goods and the joy of homemade flavors. Our journey began with a passion for crafting the perfect blend of tradition and innovation. From flaky pastries and artisan breads to custom cakes and sweet treats, each item is made with the finest ingredients and a whole lot of love. Whether you're grabbing your morning coffee and croissant, celebrating life's milestones with a personalized cake, or simply treating yourself, we're here to make your moments delicious. As a proud part of this vibrant neighborhood, we strive to create more than just baked goods—we aim to create memories. Our cozy space is where friendships are formed, stories are shared, and everyone is welcomed like family. Stop by Elm Street Bakery and experience the magic of freshly baked goodness. We can’t wait to welcome you!
